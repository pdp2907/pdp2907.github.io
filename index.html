Event Attribution prevents the reporting of erroneous conversion count

Our anonymized data revealed that for an online property with 500 or fewer daily homepage visitors, implementing event attribution significantly impacted conversion attribution. Previously, this business ranked attributed conversions as follows: paid social, CRM, and organic search (SEO). Traditionally, it was believed that paid social media had the greatest influence on converting customers, followed by CRM efforts and SEO (organic search).

However, after applying event attribution, SEO(organic search) was credited with a dramatic 400% increase in conversions. Meanwhile, paid social and CRM saw declines in attributed conversions. This exemplifies how event attribution can reshape conversion crediting, in this case recognizing SEO's outsized role for properties with limited traffic. The takeaway is that event attribution provides vital clarity into marketing channel performance.

It enabled this business to recognize SEO(organic search) and CRM as bigger drivers than previously thought. The takeaway is that event attribution is a valuable analytics tool for businesses to optimize their go-to-market strategies based on data-driven channel insights. Events provide a bird's eye view of Campaign Types & Reach

Event attribution provides insights into the reach and impact of different campaign types. Our anonymized data revealed that for awareness building campaigns at the top of the funnel, paid social and paid display performed best, achieving 37% reach. For mid-funnel consideration campaigns, CRM efforts drove the most reach at 47%. Finally, for bottom-funnel conversion campaigns, paid search and CRM generated the highest reach at 16%.

This indicates that attribution data enables clear mapping of campaign types to channels based on their ability to deliver reach. Top-funnel awareness thrives on paid social and display. Mid-funnel benefits most from CRM. And bottom-funnel conversions are optimized by integrating paid search and CRM. Event attribution quantifies the unique value of each channel by campaign objective, empowering optimized orchestration.
